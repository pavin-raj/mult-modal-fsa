# X-450 Centrifugal Pump Technical Manual (Rev 4.2)

## 1. General Specifications
- Model: X-450
- Max Flow Rate: 450 GPM (1700 L/min)
- Max Head: 180 ft (55 m)
- Motor Power: 25 HP (18.6 kW), 3-phase, 60 Hz
- Seal Type: Type 21 Mechanical Seal
- Materials: Cast iron casing, 316SS impeller

## 2. Safety Warnings
**DANGER**: Never perform maintenance on pressurized or energized equipment.

**WARNING**: Always follow company Lockout/Tagout (LOTO) procedure SOP-EL-003 before any work.

Required PPE for seal work:
- Safety glasses or face shield
- Chemical-resistant gloves
- Steel-toe safety boots
- Hearing protection

## 3. Troubleshooting

### 3.1 Excessive Vibration or Noise
Possible causes:
- Coupling misalignment
- Worn or damaged bearings
- Cavitation due to low NPSH
- Impeller imbalance or damage
- Loose foundation bolts

Diagnostic steps:
1. Measure vibration with handheld meter.
2. Listen for grinding vs. rumbling.
3. Check suction pressure and temperature.

### 3.2 Seal Leakage
**Critical**: If leakage rate exceeds 5 drops per minute, shut down immediately.

Common root causes:
- Worn seal faces
- Damaged shaft or sleeve
- Improper installation
- Excessive shaft runout (>0.002")

Seal Replacement Procedure (Section 5.2):
1. Isolate power and verify zero energy state with multimeter.
2. Close suction and discharge valves.
3. Drain pump completely.
4. Remove coupling guard and coupling.
5. Loosen seal set screws on collar.
6. Use seal puller tool (P-17) to extract old seal.
7. Inspect and clean shaft. Measure runout.
8. Install new mechanical seal (part number: SEAL-X450-22).
9. Torque set screws to 18 Nm (13 ft-lbs).
10. Reinstall coupling and guard.
11. Prime pump, open valves, and perform 10-minute run test at low load.

## 4. Maintenance Schedule
- Weekly: Visual inspection for leaks
- Monthly: Check vibration levels
- Quarterly: Lubricate bearings (if applicable)
- Annually: Full seal inspection / replacement
